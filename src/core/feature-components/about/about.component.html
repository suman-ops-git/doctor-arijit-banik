About Dr. Arijit Banik

Dr. Arijit Banik is a renowned orthopedic surgeon with over 15 years of experience in treating a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions. He completed his MBBS and MS in Orthopedics from prestigious institutions and has received advanced training in joint replacement and arthroscopic surgery.

Dr. Banik is known for his patient-centric approach, combining clinical expertise with compassion and clear communication. He has successfully performed numerous complex surgeries and is actively involved in research and medical education. His commitment to excellence and continuous learning ensures that his patients receive the best possible care using the latest techniques and technology.
